---
title: "Orpheus"
handle: "orpheus"
---

Too hot, Orpheus!  
You said spring would come again,  
not the summer heat.  

But oh, how lovely  
it is to see the shadows  
stark on the hard ground;  

to walk in the sun  
in nothing but a t-shirt  
and be comfortable.  

I would turn back too  
for a love like yours that can  
never be too much.
